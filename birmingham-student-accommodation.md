# Student Accommodation in Birmingham – Your Complete Guide

Find affordable student accommodation in Birmingham, including studios, ensuites, and shared flats. Compare prices, areas, and property types to make the right choice, ensuring the best fit for your student lifestyle.

## Price Segmentation (£80–120 / £120–160 / £160+)

### Cheap Flats (£80–120)
Budget-friendly options suitable for first-year undergraduates. Many listings include bills and are located in the City Centre for easy access to campus and amenities.

### Mid-Range (£120–160)
Comfortable flats and ensuites ideal for postgraduates and students who want a mix of affordability and convenience. Popular areas include Selly Oak.

### Premium (£160+)
High-end studios and ensuites, perfect for international students or those looking for more private space. Main areas: Edgbaston.

## Best Areas for Students

### City Centre – First-year undergraduates
Walkable area with easy access to universities, shops, and nightlife.

### Selly Oak – Postgraduates / international
Close to Birmingham University, offering a mix of studios and shared flats.

### Edgbaston – Mixed / Graduate students
Well-connected by public transport with plenty of amenities, suited for a diverse student population.

## Accommodation Types

### Studios
Private kitchen and bathroom, ideal for international students seeking independence.

### Ensuites
Private bathroom within a shared flat, mid-range pricing, suitable for postgraduates.

### Shared Flats
Affordable and social, ideal for undergraduates.

## How to Choose Student Accommodation
Consider your budget, proximity to campus, property type, and included amenities. Check if bills are included and if the flat fits your lifestyle.
