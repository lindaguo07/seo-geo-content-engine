## Price Segmentation (£80–120 / £120–160 / £160+)

### Cheap Flats (£80–120)
Budget-friendly options ideal for first-year undergraduates. Some include bills, located mainly in City Centre.

### Mid-Range (£120–160)
Suitable for postgraduates or students wanting more comfort. Popular areas include Selly Oak.

### Premium (£160+)
High-end studios and ensuites, ideal for international students. Main areas: Edgbaston.

## Best Areas for Students
- **City Centre – First-year undergraduates**: Walkable, close to campus.
- **Selly Oak – Postgraduates / international**: Near Birmingham University.
- **Edgbaston – Mixed / Graduate students**: Convenient transport and facilities.

## Accommodation Types
- **Studios**: Private kitchen and bathroom, ideal for international students.
- **Ensuites**: Private bathroom, mid-range pricing.
